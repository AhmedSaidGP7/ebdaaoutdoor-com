Artistic outdoor design solutions.

Our Company

Welcome to Ebdaa Outdoor - Technical Supervisors, an exterior design studio that specializes in creating beautiful and functional outdoor spaces. Our integrated consulting and supervision services provide design solutions for a wide range of outdoor areas, from swimming pools and fountains to landscaping, marble and stone pathways, natural and industrial wood, and modern lighting.

Our goal is to establish ourselves as a leading and distinguished consulting firm for outdoor design in Egypt and the entire region. We pride ourselves on our artistic vision and attention to detail, ensuring that each space we create is unique and reflective of our clients' personal style and needs.

Our Services

Swimming Pools

Experience the luxury and safety of our stunning swimming pools designed by our team of professionals. We prioritize your satisfaction and comfort, ensuring that each pool is aesthetically pleasing, environmentally friendly, and built to last. Our focus on smart business and well-designed spaces means that you can trust us to create the perfect outdoor oasis for your home, resort, or club.

Coordination Of Civilization

Create an outdoor space that reflects your brand's identity with our coordination of civilization service. Our team of professionals will work with you to create well-designed spaces that incorporate the latest in aesthetic trends and the highest level of safety standards. From color schemes and material selection to the shapes and relationships between elements, we prioritize your vision and satisfaction. With years of experience, we are committed to delivering smart business solutions that ensure the longevity and functionality of your outdoor space.

Landscape

Transform your outdoor space with our breathtaking landscape designs. Our team of experts will work with you to create a visually stunning and functional space that perfectly matches your style and needs. From beautiful lighting to wooden works, stone works, artificial waterfalls, and artificial turf, we strive to provide the highest quality and professional service. At Ebdaa Outdoor, we believe in smart business practices that promote sustainability and a safe environment.
