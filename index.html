Our Company

Welcome to Ebdaa Outdoor - Technical Supervisors, an exterior design studio that specializes in creating stunning and functional outdoor spaces, with a particular emphasis on swimming pools. Our integrated consulting and supervision services provide design solutions for a wide range of outdoor areas, including swimming pools, fountains, landscaping, marble and stone pathways, natural and industrial wood, and modern lighting.

Our goal is to establish ourselves as a leading and distinguished consulting firm for outdoor design in Egypt and the entire region, with swimming pools being one of our signature expertise areas. We take great pride in our artistic vision and meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that each space we create, especially swimming pools, is not only unique but also a reflection of our clients' personal style and needs.
