About Ebdaa Outdoor

Our History

Ebdaa Outdoor - Technical Supervisors was founded in 2009 as an exterior design studio that provides integrated consulting and supervision services on designs and solutions for the use of outdoor spaces. Over the years, we have established ourselves as a leading and distinguished consulting firm for outdoor design, with an artistic vision of coordinating the sites of tourist villages, including swimming pools, fountains, landscaping, marble and stone pathways, natural and industrial wood, and modern lighting in Egypt and the entire region.

We take pride in our ability to provide specialized consulting services to clients, as well as a wide range of conceptual visions designed specifically to combine art with exterior design, resulting in distinctive individual spaces.

Our Vision

At Ebdaa Outdoor, our vision is to become the premier exterior design studio in Egypt and the region. We strive to achieve this by constantly improving and expanding our services and expertise, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and innovation in our designs. We aim to be known for our ability to transform outdoor spaces into breathtakingly beautiful and functional areas that delight our clients and enhance their lifestyles.

Our Mission

Our mission at Ebdaa Outdoor is to provide integrated consulting and supervision services for outdoor design, that exceed our clients' expectations. We are committed to offering innovative and creative design solutions that cater to our clients' unique needs and preferences, while ensuring the highest level of quality and attention to detail. We aim to build lasting relationships with our clients, based on mutual trust and respect, and to continuously exceed their expectations by providing exceptional service and value. We strive to be at the forefront of our industry, constantly improving and innovating to meet the evolving needs of our clients and the market.
